Create Circles

It is not possible to manipulate vector graphics in the canvas directly. In order to reposition a graphics object, animate it, etc. it must be the property of a Shape object on the stage.

Two methods exist for creating a graphics object as a property of a shape. You must either instantiate a shape and then manipulate its graphics property, or instantiate a graphics object and pass it as an argument when you instantiate a shape.

Grow Circles

The circles grow in radius as they move out by 101%. Once the newest circle reaches a radius of 30 another is added. They are destroyed as they reach the edge of the canvas.
